About Us

Welcome to Get After It Gym

We are one of Corks newest up and coming gyms. It includes state of the art Cardio and Weight training equipment!

Our excellent qualified personal trainers and nutritionists are here to ensure that you reach your goals. Our aim is to always make client satisfaction at the top of our list of Goals and Objectives.

We intend on doing this by setting a nice, friendly and relaxed atmosphere. We are a gym where you can retain your own anonymity whilst also feeling like you are a part of the team.

We can set you up with an exercise as well as a nutritional program that can cater to suit each and every individual. This means that our trainers will design a program specifically for you at whatever stage you are in your fitness journey!
